The Event

What can you expect at Barcelona's first ever Hackfest?

classes

You'll get a crash course in web development, starting with our beginner classes led by experienced developers. We'll walk you through the basics of HTML, CSS, and Javascript and you'll apply your new skills immediately in a team project.

  • web design
  • logo design
  • cms
  • social media
01

projects

We believe in learning by doing. You and your team will choose a project brief and complete the project by the end of the day. You'll start coding from Hour 1 and learn as you go. Each team will have a teaching assistant to answer questions and keep the team on track.

  • poster design
  • logo design
  • cms
  • social media
02

people

You'll meet like-minded people who are also interested in learning a new skill. With over 500 participants, there will be people from all different backgrounds. What we all have in common is an open mind with a passion for creating.

  • web design
  • logo design
  • cms
  • social media
03

fest

In addition to learning and practicing code, you can look forward to an exciting pitch competition at the end of the day with some sweet prizes. And what kind of festival would it be without an afterparty?

04

the schedule

A breakdown of the 12-hour festival

  • Pick a project

    Meet your fellow hackers, collaborators, and partners-in-code. Get in groups and choose your project brief.

  • Start coding

    You'd be surprised how quickly you can learn to write your first lines of code. Each team member codes part of the final project.

  • Skill up

    There are classes, but don't depend on them too much. This is a different way to learn; we teach you how to teach yourself.

  • Compete

    By the end of the day, your team will have coded a full website which you can present in the pitch competition.
